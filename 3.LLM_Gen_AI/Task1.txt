1. Text-Generation ->
   *GPT-2 / GPT-Neo -
       Generates human-like text
       Completes sentences or paragraphs
       Applications-
        Chatbots
        Content writing
        Story or blog generation
        Code generation (basic)

2. Image-to-text ->
    * datalab-to/chandra -
        Chandra is an OCR model that outputs markdown, HTML, and JSON. It is highly accurate at extracting
         text from images and PDFs, while preserving layout information.

3. Text-to-image ->
    * Stable Diffusion -
       Creates images from text prompts
        Applications-
            AI art generation
            Logo & poster design
            Game and movie concept art
            Marketing visuals

4. Text Understanding ->
    * BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) -
        Understands the meaning of text
        Good at classification and question answering
        Applications -
            Sentiment analysis (positive/negative reviews)
            Spam detection
            Text classification
            Question answering systems

5. Speech Recognition ->
    * Wav2Vec 2.0
        Converts speech to text
        Applications-
            Voice assistants
            Transcription services
            Call center analytics
            Accessibility tools
